Seven tools, one app

Every video task you need, from speed adjustment to lossless concatenation. No subscription, no cloud uploads, no limits.

⚡

Speed Changer

Change playback speed from 0.5x to 4.0x with pitch preservation. No more chipmunk voices. Supports H.264, H.265, VP9, and AV1 with hardware acceleration.

  • Pitch-preserved audio
  • Hardware-accelerated
  • Batch processing
📦

Compressor

Compress video to a target file size with your choice of codec, quality, and hardware encoder. Shrink 4K footage for sharing in one click.

📷

Frame Capture

Capture single frames, batch-extract at intervals, or use automatic scene detection with adjustable sensitivity. Perfect for thumbnails and storyboards.

🔄

Remuxer

Convert between MP4, MKV, and MOV containers without re-encoding. Lossless, instant, and preserves all streams.

🎵

Audio Drop

Extract audio tracks losslessly or convert to MP3, AAC, OGG, or WAV. Drag a video, get the audio.

🔗

Video Joiner

Losslessly concatenate multiple videos with identical encoding. Reorder by drag-and-drop, check compatibility at a glance, and join in one click.

✂

Video Splitter

Cut a segment losslessly by setting start and end points. Extremely fast no-re-encode splitting. Ideal for trimming highlights or preparing clips for assembly.

  • Lossless cut
  • Precise keyframe snapping
  • Part of the Split-Edit-Join workflow
Auto-detected at launch

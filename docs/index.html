Eight tools, one app

Every video and audio task you need, from speed adjustment to lossless compression and audio conversion. No subscription, no cloud uploads, no limits.

Speed Changer

Change playback speed from 0.5x to 4.0x with pitch preservation. No chipmunk voices. Supports H.264, H.265, VP9, and AV1 with hardware acceleration.

  • Pitch-preserved audio
  • Hardware accelerated
  • Batch processing

Compressor

Compress video to a target file size with your choice of codec, quality, and hardware encoder. Shrink 4K footage for sharing in one click.

Frame Capture

Capture single frames, batch-extract at intervals, or use automatic scene detection with adjustable sensitivity. Perfect for thumbnails and storyboards.

Remuxer

Convert between MP4, MKV, and MOV containers without re-encoding. Lossless and instant. Preserves all streams.

Audio Drop

Extract audio tracks losslessly or convert to MP3, AAC, OGG, or WAV. Drag a video, get the audio.

Audio Compressor

Compress lossless audio (WAV, AIFF, FLAC, ALAC) to high-quality MP3. Choose between CBR and VBR encoding with sample rate control. Batch process multiple files.

Video Joiner

Losslessly concatenate multiple videos with identical encoding. Reorder by drag-and-drop. Check compatibility at a glance and join in one click.

Video Splitter

Cut a segment losslessly by setting start and end points. Extremely fast no-re-encode splitting. Ideal for trimming highlights or preparing clips for assembly.

  • Lossless cut
  • Keyframe snapping
  • Split-Edit-Join workflow

The idea

Scratching the itch

Good video tools exist. But finding one that is optimized for the quick, adjacent tasks — cutting without re-encoding, changing speed while preserving voice pitch, instantly extracting audio, switching containers in a click, capturing frames frame-by-frame — is surprisingly hard.

Mytory Video Tools was built to scratch that itch. It is not another video editor. It is a swiss-army knife for the moments between edits: when you need to split a clip, join segments, extract a soundtrack, or convert a format, and you want it done now, losslessly, with hardware acceleration out of the box.

No cloud uploads, no accounts, no subscriptions. Just native code, FFmpeg under the hood, and a clean interface that stays out of your way. Every operation uses sensible defaults — hardware acceleration is auto-detected, output formats are pre-optimized, and you rarely need to touch a setting to get the job done.
